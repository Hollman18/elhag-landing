Common questions

Before you call, you probably want to know…

How much does it cost to hire you?
Nothing upfront — ever. We work on contingency: our fee comes out of your settlement. If you don't win, you don't pay. The initial case review is also completely free.
Can my employer fire me for filing a claim?
Retaliation is illegal, and it's one of the claims we enforce most aggressively. You have employment protection during your case — and if your employer retaliates, that typically strengthens your case and increases your settlement.
How long will my case take?
Most of our cases settle in 4 to 9 months. Complex litigation can take longer, but we'll tell you honestly what to expect at your free review — before you commit to anything.
What if I'm not sure I have a case?
That's exactly what the free review is for. Our philosophy is unusual: if your case has poor odds, we tell you and decline it — we don't take your money to find out. You'll get an honest answer either way.
